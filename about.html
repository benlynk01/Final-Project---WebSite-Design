So what is WattsUp?

WattsUp is a small company based out of Dublin, Ireland that uses the sun as our power. Yes, we run everything off of solar panels, so we achieve 100% clean energy and power.

We have over 50 acres of solar panels in our backyard that power everything we own. Such as our factories, homes, and offices. These panels are the reason that WattsUp is the next Tesla in years to come.

Recently, WattsUp made a huge deal within international shipping. We have just recently gotten our international distribution license.

Countries we can ship to - France, United States, China, Spain, and Italy

We know it is a small amount of countries but the more we expand, the more places we will be able to ship to.
